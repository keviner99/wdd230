Target audience

Our target audience is people how loves nature and people who cares about the weather.

2 personas

The 18-year-old Juan Martinez is a student who wants to investigate the weather in his city. We can help him to understand the weather on different days for his homework.

The 50-year-old Pedro Morales is a person dedicated to agriculture in his town. He wants to protect his garden from rain. We can help him to be prepared for bad weather.

Scenarios

  1. There is a local football game and you want to go to the match with your friends. You review the Weather Channel to check if it is possible to go out or stay at home and enjoy the match.

  2. A group of students has an exposition about the weather in the city. They can use the Weather Channel to obtain accurate information and do a good performance in the expo.

  3. A friend asks you about weather predictions this week. You can use the Weather Channel on your phone to explain what does the website does for you.

  4. A group of tourists wants to get information about weather conditions so that they wear appropriately. They use the Weather Channel to get this information.

  5. There is a church activity in the outdoors. The leaders use the Weather Channel to make sure they can do this activity outdoor. If not, they can use the space inside the chapel.
