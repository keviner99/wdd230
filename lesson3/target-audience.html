Target audience

Our target audience is people how loves nature and people who cares about the weather.

2 personas

The 18-year-old Juan Martinez is a student who wants to investigate about the weather in his city. We can help him to understand the weather in different days for his homework.

The 50-year-old Pedro Morales is a person dedicated to agriculture in his town. He wants to protect his garden from rain. We can help him to be prepared for bad weather.
